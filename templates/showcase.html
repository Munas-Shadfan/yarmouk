Architecture & technology stack

How Yarmouk AI delivers enterprise-grade performance and security.

Stateful streaming agent

The core of Yarmouk AI isn't just a simple chatbot; it's a LangGraph-powered AI agent. By moving beyond linear chains, we enable complex reasoning, tool usage, and cyclic logic.

  • OAuth 2.0 security: The administration layer is secured with industry-standard JWT tokens and OAuth2 password flows.
  • Token streaming: Responses are delivered character-by-character using Server-Sent Events (SSE) for zero perceived latency.
  • Atomic checkpoints: Every turn is saved as a durable checkpoint in PostgreSQL, allowing for perfect state recovery.
